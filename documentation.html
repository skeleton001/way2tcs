Documentation


Here is the list of all Documents required to be submitted at the time of joining

# Documents Details
1 Offer Letter Print Out (1 copy)
2 Joining Letter Print Out (1 copy)
3 Birth Certificate 1 Photo Copy [An Affadavit if applicable.]
4 Passport 1 Photocopy
5 Voter ID/ Adhar Card 1 Photocopy
6 Address Proof 1 Photocopy
7 10th Marksheet & Certificate 1 Photocopy of each
8 12th Marksheet & Certificate 1 photocopy of each
9 Diploma Certificate & all sem Marksheet 1 Photocopy each
10 Degree Certificate and all sem Marksheet 1 Photocopy of each
11 Service Agreement 1st page on 100 Rs stamp Paper & remaining on plain A4 pages and then fill the blanks with Pen (Blue/Black)
12 Surety Certificate Last page of Service Agreement
13 Surety Documents Passport or (PAN card + form no 16) {Land Leed & FD has been discontinued}
14 Non-Criminal Affidavit Notarised Affidavit on 100 Rs Stamp paper
15 Medical Certificate 1 Original Copy
16 NSR e-card 1 Print out

Points to be noted while preparing documents for ILP:

  • Passport, PAN card is mandatory document.
  • Attestation of Documents
    • Only 1 attested copy of all the documents (photocopies) with the original document has to be submitted at the time of ILP.
      Original documents will be returned on the same teh same day
    • Any one out of the below mentioned authorities can attested the documents:
      1. Manager of the nationalized bank where the candidate holds an account.
      2. District Collector of the district in which the candidate resides.
      3. Gazette officers (for more info visit www.egazette.nic.in)
      4. Tehsildar of the village.
    • Special documents:
      1. Medical Certificate- Attested by any private or government MBBS Doctor possessing valid Registration number.
      2. Surety verification form
      3. Offer letter and Joining letter need to be self attested (i.e. signed by candidate.) {This can be done at ILP also}
  • Notarization of Documents:
    • Non-criminal Affidavit (Prescribed format is provided in Joining Kit on Next step portal)
    • Birth Affidavit / Address affidavit / Multiple name affidavit / Multiple signature affidavit (If applicable)
      (You need to produce these notarized affidavit if any of the documents has mistakes or some missing information, there is no prescribed format by TCS, advocate will guide you better.)
  • Service Agreement
    • Service agreement should not be notarized. However if you have got it notarized, the document would still suffice.
    • If Rs 100 stamp paper is not available, you can prepare the SA on two Rs 50 stamp or five Rs 20 stamp paper.
    • Print SA as given in the joining annexture without any changes.
    • Blank in the SA can be filled in either with a blue or black pen.
    • You may use whitener only for 3-4 rectifications.
  • Surety
    • A Surety can be a family member or person known to you provided he fulfill the below criteria:
      Surety can either be an individual who is an Income Tax payee or an individual who possesses landed property. A retired employee filing an Income Tax Return & possessing a pan card can also be a surety.
    • Either current assessment year or previous assessment year It Return can only be submitted for surety.
  • Documents to be Submitted
    • All the documents which you need submit on the day of the day of your joining should be only in English. (If any particular document is not in English, just translate it in English on Rs 100 stamp paper, no need to notarized. Eg: If Birth Certificate is in Hindi, just translate it on Rs 100 stamp paper, there is no prescribed format given by TCS)
  • Medical Certificate:
    • It must be prepared within 1 month prior to your Joining


    • All affidavits must be on Rs 100 stamp paper, whatsoever!!!
