Selection Process

Hello... Let's have detailed discussion on Selection process...

Assuming you are having hall-ticket to be a part of Off-campus Drive, let's dive into Selection Process.

TCS follows a modern and well designed Selection Process which includes

     Online Aptitude Exam
     Technical Interview
     HR + Managerial Interview

Fortunately for Off-campus Drive, these are not conducted on a single date. First an Online Exam is organized in different schedules in more than 40 cities, simultaneously. Within few days result of Online Exam will be declared on Next Step {Campus Commune} Portal as well as an e-mail will be sent to all shortlisted students.
And within next 5 days you will receive date & location for next two stages i.e. Interview Process via mail.

There is no GD round! [For In-Campus Drive, all rounds may be conducted in one or two days, quite lucky! ]



Online Aptitude Exam

As I said TCS uses well designed process; it starts reflecting from here itself. Unlike traditional Apti Exam (All shity apti+verbal English), in TCS online exam there is e-mail writing + Aptitude.

Why e-mail writing?

Because in future, almost for every work you have to write an e-mail on TCS internal server. So it is included here.
One e-mail and around 30 questions (with negative marking) may be there in apti with time limit of One and half hour. Believe me, this is more than sufficient to write an e-mail (10 minutes) and solve all 30 questions (80 minutes) unlike other shity apti exams. To get positive results, you don't need to have hi-fi apti practice, rather a logical approach to solve any problem is needed. As more than sufficient time is given, it is obvious that cut-off will be too high. Yes, generally 20 to 25 questions should be correct to reach upto cut-off in Off-Campus Drive. In a particular region/zone around 150 to 200 students (depending on company's current requirements) out off 2000 to 3000 (depending on location/region) are selected, isn't it fascinating? Yet another fact, in a year, more than 15,000 students are selected throughout the Nation.
After clearing Online Apti, here comes Interview Process:
If you are in this stage, believe me half war is over. Remaining is just a cheery on the cake! This process comprises of Technical Interview and HR Interview (some times Managerial Interview also).

Technical Interview:

I really don't understand, why the hell all Tech HR managers appear like the most Innocent Tech persons on the Planet... Really!!! So polite, humble and innocent

but don't go on their faces, Please! This is where chances of getting out of the race are more. However, TCS is the place where any one, who holds passion of programming or having high will power to learn programming or a damn good Programmer can stay alive, It really doesn't matter where the hell you are from, what the hell your branch is!!! It's true. If you have little good command on just C and/or C++ or any other programming language (just to showcase in front of Tech HR, at least) then you are SELECTED!. Other thing is just an icing on the cake. However, it completely depends on Tech HR panel, whether your interview will be simple or toughest of the session. So, Give your best shot!!!

Very Imp Note: You should have detailed knowledge of your academic project; you won't believe, my interview was stretched for half an hour only on my Project



HR Interview

I know many of you have already been trained for interview, if not; don't worry. Just follow basic common sense. This works!!! Really!!!
for example, most famous question in TCS HR interview:

What will you do even being in TCS; IBM, FB, GOOGLE offer you a job?

Most of guys & girls said,

"No sir/ma'am I'll still join TCS, TATA ka namak khaya hai na!!!"

Please don't try be over smart! They can just F**k you in seconds!!! Instead you may say (as I said),

"Definetely I'll join them, provided they are paying more than you do!"

This is just a common sense and true human behavior.



And please please please be true at each and every moment, even if they ask you about Girlfriend or Boyfried!!! Yes, tell them truth, since you have not done any Crime by having girlfriend/boyfriend.
In one of my friends' HR Interview, she(HR) asked, after few common questions;

Why TCS?

My friend just replied,

"Ma'am, Paise kamana hai!!!"

Done! Really!!! Thats the only thing she asked. He got selected!

This may not work every time, use your own creativity!



Important thing to remember, as I Said TCS is completely Digitized, at every stage you will be awarded with some scores and few comments by different HRs, generally more than two HRs at each stage; and this data is circulated online to next stage. So, Personal HR Interviewer always has your whole Kundali in front of him/her. So, someone's selection is not just dependent on Apti, Tech Interview or Personal Interview.
Let me justify this, my own HR was so fu**ed up after a point. My Resume had more than four spelling mistakes, and most unfortunately, she(HR) saw that at first glance!!! OMG! My chances were reduced to 10-20 % but thanks to my Apti and Tech HR; She(HR) told me in clear words; "It's just your Apti and Tech HR can save you now!!!". Ok ok, this is not so clear but still... And, whola!!! I got Selected...
So in short, Interview is not at all a horror place. It will not be horror place if you stop speaking lie and start using your common sense instead Engineering Dimag!
And please, InTech HR, use Technical Dimag

Guys, be ready for a long hectic day; you may feel frustrated at the end of it.

My interview location was about 60 km aways from my base location. I slept at 1am, woke up at 5am and catched local transport at 6 am. Somehow I reached Interview location just 5min before 9 am. But I was so unfortunate, my Tech HR was conducted at 5pm and Personal HR at 7pm. And I reached back my base location at 11pm that too by local transport!

So be ready for such situations... Have some good breakfast in morning.

Result of Whole Interview process will not be declared on same date.
You will be asked to check your mail regularly. If you could manage to clear all stages successfully... An Offer letter will be directly sent to you by mail and will also be available on Next Step portal...
CONGRATULATIONS... Cheers!!!
Yes now you are a part of TCS, but external of course. Now to get on board as early as possible, TCS provides a short cut!!!
