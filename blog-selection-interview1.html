My Interview @ TCS...

Blog / Selection Process / By Guest writer / 25 Dec 2015

Hello friends,

Action is foundational key to all success.
- Pablo Picasa

Actually there nothing much to be shared because my interview was coolest interview happened in my campus.

First and foremost thing you have to remember is, score max to max in aptitude test so that further tasks become simpler.

So talking about my Technical Interview, I was asked few questions related to my project which were quite easy to answer as I was thorough with it because i made it by myself from scratch. Then they asked me some theory questions based on C language and finally they asked me to write a program in C language to print the digits 1 - 9 sequentially. Thats it. Tooo damn easy.

In Personal Interview, they asked me about my interests and what I do to enhance in my favorite fields. Thereafter, I said "I like to know the new technological gadgets", they asked me about the specifications and properties of the electronic gadgets that I use in my daily life including my smart-phone.
Then focusing on my resume, they demanded for the working of bots I presented in techfest (IIT Bombay) and various competitions in my engineering life. And done!!!

I'm now enjoying Awesome life @ TCS family.

All the best guys!!!
