Getting into TCS...

Hello friends, without wasting the time let's dive into the process of Getting into TCS.
TCS conducts TWO Off-Campus Drives every year; one excusively for passout students generally in December and other for all (passouts + final year students) generally in February & In-Campus to chalte rehata!!!
Everyone appearing for Off-Campus or In-Campus Drives has to go through following 4 stages:

Wait wait!!! Don't get panic, I'll explain each and every stage one by one in detail.
TCS has divided the recruitment process into 4 stages. To get on bord in TCS you have to clear each and every stage succesfully! So, to make it easier for you, I've divided the whole site in 4 main pages under 'Process' tab. Don't get confused with the terminologies, as we go along you will get to know everything.
Now let's begin with very first process i.e. Registration

Registration

This is where you Register yourself on TCS Next-Step online Portal

Selection

Selection process includes Online Apti, Tech. Interview & HR Interview

Pre-ILP

This is where you have to complete an Online Course to get on board as early as possible.

ILP

Initial Learning Program, this is where your training starts! And you are in TCS!!!
